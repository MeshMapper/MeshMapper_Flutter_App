## Full Description

### iOS App Store / Google Play Store

**Map the mesh. Build the network. Join the community.**

MeshMapper is the official companion app for MeshCore mesh network devices. Connect your MeshCore radio via Bluetooth, send GPS-tagged pings across the mesh, and contribute real-time coverage data to the global MeshMapper community map.

**KEY FEATURES**

**Seamless Device Connection**
Connect to your MeshCore device via Bluetooth Low Energy with automatic device detection. Supports 30+ device models including Ikoka, Heltec, RAK, LilyGo, Seeed, and more. Auto-configures transmit power settings for optimal performance and device safety.

**Real-Time Mesh Mapping**
Send wardriving pings and track which mesh repeaters echo your signal. See your coverage contributions on the live map as they're uploaded to the MeshMapper API.

**Intelligent Signal Tracking**
- TX Mode: Track your transmitted pings and see how far they travel through the mesh
- RX Mode: Passively monitor mesh traffic and log repeater observations
- Auto Mode: Hands-free pinging at configurable distance intervals

**Offline-Ready**
Coverage data is queued locally and automatically uploaded when connectivity is available. Never lose your mapping data.

**Community Contribution**
Every ping you send helps build a comprehensive coverage map of the MeshCore mesh network. See your contributions alongside the global community's mapping efforts.

**SUPPORTED DEVICES**
- Ikoka: Stick, Nano, Handheld (22-33dBm variants)
- Heltec: V2, V3, V4, Wireless Tracker, MeshPocket
- RAK: 4631, 3x72
- LilyGo: T-Echo, T-Deck, T-Beam, T-LoRa
- Seeed: Wio E5, T1000, Xiao variants
- And many more...

**REQUIREMENTS**
- MeshCore-compatible mesh radio device
- Bluetooth Low Energy support
- Location services enabled for GPS tagging
- Internet connection for uploading coverage data

**PRIVACY**
MeshMapper only collects GPS coordinates when you actively choose to send pings. Location data is used solely for mesh coverage mapping and is contributed to the community map.
